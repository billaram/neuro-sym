# Bibliography

## Week 1 — Foundations

1. **DARPA — The Third Wave of AI**
   - Source: DARPA Perspective on AI
   - Key topics: Wave-1 (handcrafted), Wave-2 (statistical), Wave-3 (contextual)
   - PDF: `research/papers/week1/`

2. **Garcez et al. — Neural-Symbolic Learning and Reasoning: A Survey and Interpretation**
   - Key topics: Integration of neural and symbolic AI, taxonomy of approaches
   - PDF: `research/papers/week1/`

3. **Reed & de Freitas — Neural Programmer-Interpreters (2016)**
   - Key topics: Learning to compose programs, neural execution
   - PDF: `research/papers/week1/`

4. **Wei et al. — Chain-of-Thought Prompting Elicits Reasoning in Large Language Models (2022)**
   - Key topics: Step-by-step reasoning, prompting strategies
   - PDF: `research/papers/week1/`

## Week 2 — Parsing & IR

5. **Semantic Parsing — Text-to-SQL Survey**
   - Key topics: Natural language to structured query, grammar-based decoding
   - PDF: `research/papers/week2/`

6. **Program Synthesis with LLMs**
   - Key topics: Code generation, specification-guided synthesis
   - PDF: `research/papers/week2/`

7. **Microsoft PROSE — Program Induction**
   - Key topics: Input-output example driven synthesis, FlashFill
   - PDF: `research/papers/week2/`

## Week 3 — Symbolic Reasoning

8. **Evans & Grefenstette — Learning Explanatory Rules through Neural Satisfaction (2018)**
   - Key topics: Differentiable ILP, rule learning
   - PDF: `research/papers/week3/`

9. **Fedus et al. — Switch Transformers (2021)**
   - Key topics: Conditional computation, mixture of experts
   - PDF: `research/papers/week3/`

10. **Guu et al. — REALM: Retrieval-Augmented Language Model Pre-Training (2020)**
    - Key topics: Retrieval-augmented reasoning, knowledge grounding
    - PDF: `research/papers/week3/`

## Week 4 — Integration

11. **Reed & de Freitas — Neural Programmer-Interpreters (deep dive)**
    - Revisit with focus on execution architecture
    - PDF: `research/papers/week4/`

12. **Retrieval-First Architectures (survey)**
    - Key topics: RAG patterns, retrieval before generation
    - PDF: `research/papers/week4/`

13. **LangGraph / Semantic Kernel — Comparison Study**
    - Key topics: Agent orchestration frameworks, graph-based pipelines
    - PDF: `research/papers/week4/`
